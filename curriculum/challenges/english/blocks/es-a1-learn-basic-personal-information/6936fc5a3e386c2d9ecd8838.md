# --description--

In the question `¿Quieres que te lo deletree?`, several elements work together to express an offer.

`Quieres que` is used to ask if someone wants something.

`Te` refers to the person being spoken to. In this case, Camila.

`Lo` refers to the information, here Mateo's email.

`Deletree` comes from `deletrear`, which means to spell a word letter by letter.

Together, the question is used to offer to spell the email to Camila.
